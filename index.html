Barak Itkin

Masters student for Computer Science in Tel Aviv University, doing my research in the Deep Learning Lab under the guidance of Prof. Lior Wolf. My research is part of the ArchAIDE project, which is included in the European Union's 2020 horizon program. The research focuses on building an automatic classification system pottery sherds, from simple 2D images.

Aside from studying, I work at Magic Leap as a Software/Algorithm Engineer, TA the Introduction to Information Security course, dance and teach West Coast Swing (WCS) at Media West, debate in the TAU debate club, and run long distances.
